Lamborghini Lineup

Ferruccio Lamborghini already had two factories in Italy, one producing tractors and the other air conditioners, when he decided to take on Enzo Ferrari in the automotive realm. Lamborghini felt that Ferrari's cars were too Spartan, too noisy, and too close to the track cars. He wanted to build performance cars with interior refinements that equaled the technology of the engine.

Lamborghini Lineup

In 1963, Lamborghini started production on his dream cars, with the 350 GTV making its debut at the Turin auto show that year.
